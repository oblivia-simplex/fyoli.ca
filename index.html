Macarons, Gay

A selection of macarons filled with a variety of flavours, made from fresh, natural ingredients, including pistachio and Arabic gum, lavender and strawberry, pineapple, lime and ginger, sage and orange, and carrot, cinnamon and burnt butter. The shell is made from almond flour and whipped egg whites, so our macarons are always gluten-free.
