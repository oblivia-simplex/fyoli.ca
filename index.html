about us

fyoli is a small, independent bakery that specializes in delicious confections and desserts. we're based in sackville, new brunswick and halifax, nova scotia. find us at the sackville farmers market, or at one of our special events!

tasty treats

banana bavarian cream

A light and fluffy bavarian cream with caramelized bananas and hints of tea and ginger, topped with homemade caramel corn.

macarons, gay

A selection of macarons filled with a variety of flavours, made from fresh, natural ingredients, including pistachio and Arabic gum, lavender and strawberry, pineapple, lime and ginger, sage and orange, and carrot, cinnamon and burnt butter. The shell is made from almond flour and whipped egg whites, so our macarons are always gluten-free.
